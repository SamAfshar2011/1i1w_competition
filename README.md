# 🏗️ ANKA – AI-Powered Earthquake Structural Safety Assistant

> **Gold Medal Project – 1Idea1World (1I1W) International Competition**

ANKA is an AI-powered structural safety assistant designed to help users better understand the potential impact of earthquakes on buildings through intelligent risk assessment, visual simulation, personalized recommendations, and AI-assisted guidance.

Rather than replacing professional structural engineering evaluations, ANKA provides an **AI-generated preliminary assessment** to improve public awareness, preparedness, and decision-making before and after earthquakes.

---

# ✨ Features

## 📊 AI Collapse Risk Prediction

Users enter eight structural and seismic parameters describing their building.

The AI model analyzes the information and estimates the **probability of structural collapse** during an earthquake.

The prediction is presented as a percentage together with an intuitive color-coded risk indicator.

---

## 🟢 Color-Coded Risk Visualization

To make the prediction easier to understand, ANKA converts numerical probabilities into visual safety levels.

| Collapse Probability | Risk Level | Color |
|---------------------|-----------|-------|
| 0–25% | Low Risk | 🟢 Green |
| 26–50% | Moderate Risk | 🟡 Yellow |
| 51–75% | High Risk | 🟠 Orange |
| 76–100% | Critical Risk | 🔴 Red |

This allows users to immediately understand the severity of the predicted risk.

---

## 💡 Personalized Safety Recommendations

Based on the predicted risk and building characteristics, ANKA generates intelligent recommendations such as:

- Structural strengthening suggestions
- Earthquake preparedness guidance
- Preventive safety measures
- Building maintenance recommendations

Recommendations are generated using predefined engineering rules designed to improve user awareness.

---

## 🤖 ANKA AI Assistant

ANKA includes an integrated AI chatbot specialized in:

- Earthquake safety
- Structural engineering concepts
- Building preparedness
- Interpretation of prediction results

The chatbot is powered through the Claude Sonnet model via API and can access the user's building information to provide contextual assistance.

---

---

## 🎨 Customizable User Interface

Users can personalize the application by changing:

- Primary Color
- Accent Color

This allows a more personalized user experience while preserving accessibility.

---

## 🌎 2D Earthquake Simulation

ANKA includes an educational two-dimensional earthquake simulator.

Users can enter:

- Building information
- Earthquake characteristics

The simulator visually demonstrates how the building may respond under the specified earthquake scenario, helping users better understand potential structural behavior.

---

## 📄 Secure PDF Report Generation

Every prediction can be exported as a professional PDF report.

Each report contains:

- Building information
- Predicted collapse probability
- Risk level
- Safety recommendations
- Unique Report ID
- Verification hologram
- Generation timestamp

Reports are intended for documentation and preliminary assessment purposes.

---

## ✅ PDF Verification System

Every generated report is registered inside the project's verification database.

Users can verify a report by entering its information inside the verification page.

The system checks whether the report exists and confirms whether it is authentic or invalid.

# ⚠️ Important Disclaimer

ANKA is designed as an **AI-assisted structural safety support system**.

The predictions, recommendations, simulations, and reports generated by ANKA are intended for educational, informational, and preliminary assessment purposes only.

ANKA does **not** replace professional structural engineering inspections, certified engineering reports, or official governmental safety evaluations.

Critical engineering or legal decisions should always be made in consultation with qualified structural engineers and relevant authorities.
